Fangzhou You

Biography

Fangzhou is a postdoctoral researcher at the Dyson School of Design Engineering, Imperial College London.
Her current research interests include participatory design, user behaviour research, design for behaviour change and design for susutainable behaviour.
She is a versatile scholar in terms of her educational experiences. She hold PhD from Loughborough University, MA from Interaction Design (Tongji University, China), MSc from Systemic Design (Polytechnic University of Turin, Italy), BA in IndustrIal Design (East China Normal University, China) and BSc in Applied Psychology (Ibid.).
